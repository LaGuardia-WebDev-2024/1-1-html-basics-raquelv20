Bears 101

Basic Info

Bears are large mammals found in mainly the Northern Hemisphere and some parts of the Southern Hemisphere. They have thick fur and strong claws with species that range from polar bears to grizzly bears. Most bears are omnivores with diets like plants, fish, and small animals although some,like the polar bear,are more carnivorous.
